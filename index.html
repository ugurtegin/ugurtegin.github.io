About me

I received my BSc degree in Physics and my MSc degree in Material Science and Nanotechnology from Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, in 2015 and 2018, respectively. I am pursuing my PhD in The Doctoral Program in Photonics at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), Lausanne, Switzerland.

My current research interests include optical computing, spatiotemporal nonlinear optics and mode-locked lasers. In other words, I generate high power ultrashort pulses and use them for various applications such as optical computing, optical neural networks and wavelength generation.
